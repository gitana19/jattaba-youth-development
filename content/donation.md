# Support Our Mission

Your generous donations help us continue our vital work in the community. Every contribution, no matter the size, makes a real difference in the lives of youth and families.

## How Your Donation Helps

- **$25** - Provides school supplies for one student
- **$50** - Supports healthcare services for a family
- **$100** - Funds a week of educational programs
- **$500** - Contributes to infrastructure projects like the borehole or clinic
- **$1000+** - Enables major community development initiatives
