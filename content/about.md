# Jattaba Youth Development Association (JYDA)

The Jattaba Youth Development Association (JYDA) is dedicated to empowering young individuals through various programs and initiatives aimed at fostering personal growth, community engagement, and skill development. Our mission is to create a supportive environment where youth can thrive, learn, and contribute positively to society.

## Our Vision

We envision a world where every young person has the opportunity to realize their full potential and make meaningful contributions to their communities.

## Our Mission

Our mission is to provide resources, mentorship, and opportunities that enable youth to develop essential life skills, pursue their passions, and become active members of their communities.

## Core Values

- **Empowerment:** We believe in empowering youth to take charge of their futures.
- **Inclusivity:** We promote an inclusive environment where all voices are heard and valued.
- **Collaboration:** We work together with community partners to maximize our impact.
- **Integrity:** We uphold the highest standards of integrity in all our actions.
- **Tradition:** We honor our cultural and religious heritage ensuring the continuity of our traditions for future generations.
- **Environmental Protection:** We are committed to protecting our environment creating a sustainable future for our community.

Join us in our journey to uplift and inspire the youth of today for a better tomorrow!
